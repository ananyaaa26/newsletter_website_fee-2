American singer-songwriter Dave Loggins dies

Dave Loggins was a country singer and songwriter. He died aged 76 on Wednesday in Nashville in hospice care. Born and raised in Tennessee, Dave was “one of the state's most notable songwriters,” says American celebrity news website TMZ. He wrote hits for Johnny Cash, Toby Keith, Alabama, Wynonna Judd, Reba McEntire, Willie Nelson, Kenny Rogers, Ray Charles, Smokey Robinson, and Three Dog Night.
