Twelfth of July Parades takes place
over Northern Ireland

The Twelfth of July celebrates the Glorious Revolution because it helped birth the freedom of the press, the right to a fair trial by an independent judiciary, and a modern banking system, the leader of Orangeism has said. Orange Order Grand Master Edward Stevenson issued his comments on the eve of the annual Twelfth of July celebrations in 18 parades across Northern Ireland. “The Twelfth of July is upon us once again and what other event can compare to this glorious celebration?” he asked, in a written address.
